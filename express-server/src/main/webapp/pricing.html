Pricing

Community / Open Source - Free!

Express will always be free for open source projects and projects with a user base of 5 or less. Redredred makes extensive use of open source frameworks and feels strongly that it's products should remain free to the open source community.

Small projects are also welcome to use Express free of charge. If the project accepts more than 5 workers including the project admin, then all users must pay the standard rate. Also if project workers join a second project then they must also pay the standard fee.

Individual - $7/month or $60/year

Individual license is required for a user when their project team is larger than five people or a team member wants to work on multiple projects. Team members with this license still count toward the 5 worker limit on a project, however they are free to work on, or manage, as many projects as they like.

Enterprise - $5/month or $50/year

The Enterprise license is available when 20 or more licenses are bought at the same time. Under this license users can manage any number of projects and have any noumber of workers on their projects.

This license represents excellent value for companies which are using Express across all development.

Free Usage!

Express is currently offering the use of our service absolutely free while we get things off the ground and increase our feature base. This introductory free period will likely come to an end around the Q4 2009.

Even once Express moves to a paid model, you'll always get a month free to try us out, plus certain usage levels of our service will always be free! So please take advantage of this period and try out our service, we think you will be happy to stay on even when your usage gets to the point of requiring payment.
